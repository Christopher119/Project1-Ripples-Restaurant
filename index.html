About Us

We are situated in a stunning location just over the shores of Mulroy Bay, offering gorgeous views with the option for indoor and outdoor dining. We can accommodate large or small social events, corporate dinners and parties of up to 120 seated guests here, suitable for any range of events from birthday parties, to christenings, and weddings. All wedding receptions are personally supervised by proprietors Des and Louise Mc Gettigan who assist with every detail necessary and our menus are specifically developed with the bride and grooms preference and budget in mind.

Banks of Mulroy Bay, Kerrykeel, Co. Donegal
